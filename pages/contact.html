Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent matters, please use the live chat or phone support during business hours.

Do you offer one-on-one tutoring?

Yes, we offer personalized tutoring sessions. Please mention your interest in one-on-one tutoring in your message, and we'll provide details about availability and pricing.

Can I suggest a topic for a tutorial?

Absolutely! We welcome topic suggestions from our community. Please use the feedback category when submitting your message, and we'll consider your suggestion for future content.

Are your courses free?

We offer both free and premium content. Our beginner tutorials are completely free, while advanced courses and personalized support are part of our premium offerings.
